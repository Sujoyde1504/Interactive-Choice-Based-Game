Kingdom of Alarion

In the kingdom of Alarion, nestled amidst the towering peaks and ancient forests, peace reigned for centuries under the rule of King Alaric. His only son, Prince Rodrick, was known throughout the land for his valor and kindness. By his side was his childhood friend, Valen, a skilled swordsman and strategist. The two had been inseparable since they could remember, facing every challenge together, whether it was hunting in the dense woods or sparring under the shadow of the castle walls.
